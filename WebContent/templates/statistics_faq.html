Statistics Help

    1. How to analyze the Statistics?

      2. Wait 3 seconds for The Pie to be seen | A Pie Chart Will appear | Every chunk has a label on it represent the usage each room consume over the last month
      For brief analysis -> on the bottom there is a Line declaring Total consumption of your house over the last month

    2. How To see statistics per single room

      3. Click on menu On top right | Statistics | Click on room name

    3. Is it a fixed timeframe?

      4. House statistics are shown per last month.
      However Statistics per single room can be seen by 1Day/1Weeek/1Month

    4. How do i change the timeframe per room?

      5. Click on menu On top right | Statistics | Click on room name
      On top center | Choose from 1D = Day / 1W = Week/ 1M= Month

    5. How do i read Bars Chart

      6. vertical scale per Volt usage | Every bar will show you how much volt did the device used
