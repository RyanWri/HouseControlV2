Devices Help

    1. How To Create new Device?

      2. Click on menu On top right | Rooms Management | choose a room | Create Device

    2. How To set the device type

      3. On the device create form you have a drop down Menu "Device Type"| Choose from the menu

    3. How do i add a device to my room?

      4. Click on menu On top right | Rooms Management | choose a room |On the bottom Dropown menu "Choose Device to add" | click "ADD DEVICE TO ROOM"

    4. I want to turn on/off my device

      5. Within the room view | Choose your device | Turn on/off

    5. I want the same device in different rooms, can i do that?

      6. Off course!!! You can add same device to multiple rooms
